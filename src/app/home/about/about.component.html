About Us

Many people find it very difficult to publish articles in the newspapers and difficult to know what they can publish. Therefore, this website is designed to make this process easier for users

Ethics policy All journalists and editors have signed the “Code of Ethics of the Norwegian Press” and have committed to abide by its strict journalistic principles. We need to be a trustworthy news source that our ethical standards reflect. The editors of AZ News.com have also signed the “Rights and duties of the editor” by Nored. We do recognize the time-critical industry we inhabit may lead to publishing errors that are promptly rectified. We do communicate clearly with editors and journalists if breaches of the ethical code are found. Fact-checking and verification We demand that journalists and editors ensure that they report facts correctly and that each story is verifiable.
